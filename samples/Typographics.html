1. -- is rendered as —:

    This — that.

  2. (c) or (C) is rendered as ©:

    This © that.

  3. (r) or (R) is rendered as ®:

    This ® that.

  4. (tm) or (TM) is rendered as ™:

    This ™ that.

  5. <- is rendered as ←:

    This ← that; this ← that;

  6. -> is rendered as →:

    This → that; this → that;

  7. " is rendered as “ or &rdquo:

    ”This” and “that”.
